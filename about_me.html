I graduated from high school in Darwin where I studied mostly STEM subjects and represented the school in math and science competitions. During my time at school I also played music performing on Clarinet in the youth orchestras and concert bands. I then went onto university studying Electronic Engineering I dropped at the end of 2nd year to help support my parents. I went on to work in electronic retail and started learning sound engineering I eventually pursued a career in corporate AV specialising in Video systems.

I’ve had many hobbies in addition to being semi professional musician playing clarinet and saxophone, I enjoy boards games, computer games, camping and cooking. The hobby I’m most passionate about is scuba diving. Due to its proximity to Darwin I’ve spent a lot of time underwater off the coast of Bali and Lombok. I've also spent a bit of time diving around South Australian costal waters.

My priorities in life are

  • Enjoy life
  • Finish a Bachelor of Computer Science
  • Work on my own projects while pursuing a career in development.
